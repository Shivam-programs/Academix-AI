# 🎓 AI-Powered Education Intelligence Platform

> An AI-first Learning Management System (LMS) that helps educational institutions create, manage, and deliver personalized learning experiences through Artificial Intelligence.

---

# 📖 Overview

The **AI-Powered Education Intelligence Platform** is a next-generation educational ecosystem designed for schools, colleges, coaching institutes, and online educators.

Unlike traditional Learning Management Systems (LMS), this platform integrates Artificial Intelligence into every stage of teaching and learning.

Institutions can:

- Create organizations
- Manage teachers and students
- Publish free or paid courses
- Conduct live and recorded classes
- Upload learning materials
- Create assignments and quizzes
- Track student performance
- Receive AI-powered educational insights

Students receive personalized learning experiences with AI-generated recommendations, intelligent tutoring, automatic evaluations, and detailed progress tracking.
